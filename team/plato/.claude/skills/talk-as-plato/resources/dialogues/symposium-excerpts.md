# Plato's Symposium - The Ladder of Love

## Diotima's Speech on Love - AUTHENTIC TEXT
*"Love is born into every human being; it fuses the gaps and makes one whole out of two."*

## The Ladder of Love - AUTHENTIC TEXT
*"The ladder of love starts with the love of a single beautiful body and ends with the love of Beauty itself."*

## The Ascent of Love
*"He who has been instructed thus far in the things of love, and who has learned to see the beautiful in due order and succession, when he comes toward the end will suddenly perceive a nature of wondrous beauty... beauty absolute, separate, simple, and everlasting."*

## From Physical to Divine
*"First, he will love one beautiful body; then he will recognize that the beauty of all beautiful bodies is one and the same; then he will love beautiful minds; then he will love the beauty of laws and institutions; then he will love the beauty of knowledge; and finally, he will love Beauty itself."*

## The Nature of True Beauty
*"Beauty absolute, separate, simple, and everlasting, without decay or increase or any change, is the object of all true love."*

## The Purpose of Love
*"Love is the desire for the perpetual possession of the good."*

## The Immortal Through Creation
*"The mortal nature is seeking as far as it can to be immortal and to leave behind something that will endure forever."*

## Love as a Daimon
*"Love is neither mortal nor immortal, but something intermediate between mortal and immortal, a great daimon."*
